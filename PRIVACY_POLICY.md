# Privacy Policy for Trek

**Effective Date**: December 29, 2025
**Last Updated**: December 29, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Trek ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Trek mobile application (the "App").

By using Trek, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

**Account Information**:
- Email address (required for account creation)
- Display name (optional)
- Profile photo (optional)
- Unit preferences (metric/imperial)

**Activity Data**:
- Activity names and descriptions
- Activity type (running, cycling, walking, hiking)
- Start and end times
- Notes or comments you add to activities

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

**Location Data**:
- GPS coordinates during activity recording
- Route paths and waypoints
- Elevation data
- Location accuracy information

**Note**: Location data is only collected when you actively record an activity. We do not track your location in the background unless you are recording.

**Activity Metrics**:
- Distance traveled
- Duration
- Pace and speed
- Elevation gain/loss
- Split times

**Device Information**:
- Device model and operating system version
- App version
- Unique device identifier (for analytics)
- Language and region settings

**Usage Data**:
- App interactions and feature usage
- Crash reports and error logs
- Performance metrics

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not access your contacts
- We do not access your photo library (except when you choose to upload a profile photo)
- We do not track your location when the app is not in use
- We do not collect payment information (app is free)
- We do not collect health data from Apple Health

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Primary Functions
1. **Activity Tracking**: Record and display your fitness activities
2. **Route Visualization**: Show your routes on a map
3. **Statistics**: Calculate and display your performance metrics
4. **Account Management**: Manage your user account and profile

### Secondary Functions
5. **App Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to improve features
6. **Bug Fixes**: Identify and fix technical issues
7. **Customer Support**: Respond to your questions and support requests
8. **Security**: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access

### What We Do NOT Do
- We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
- We do NOT use your location data for advertising
- We do NOT share your activity data publicly without your consent
- We do NOT send marketing emails (unless you opt-in)

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
- **Cloud Storage**: Your data is stored securely on Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) servers
- **Local Storage**: Activity data is temporarily cached on your device for offline access
- **Server Location**: Data is stored in secure data centers (location depends on your region)

### Security Measures
- All data transmission is encrypted using TLS/SSL
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- Firebase Authentication provides secure user authentication
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and data encryption at rest

### Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Data is retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Inactive Accounts**: Accounts inactive for 2+ years may be deleted after notification

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Share Data With:

**Service Providers**:
- **Firebase/Google**: For authentication, database, and storage services
- **Apple**: For Apple Sign In authentication (if you use this option)

These providers have access only to perform services on our behalf and are obligated to maintain data confidentiality.

### We Do NOT Share Data With:
- Advertisers or ad networks
- Data brokers
- Social media platforms (unless you explicitly share)
- Other third-party apps

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:
- Valid legal processes (subpoena, court order)
- Protecting our rights, property, or safety
- Preventing fraud or security issues
- Enforcing our Terms of Service

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### Access and Portability
- **View Your Data**: Access all your activity data within the app
- **Export Your Data**: Download all your activities in GPX format
- **Data Portability**: Transfer your data to another service

### Control Your Data
- **Update Information**: Edit your profile and activity details anytime
- **Privacy Settings**: Control activity visibility (public/private)
- **Delete Activities**: Remove individual activities
- **Delete Account**: Permanently delete your account and all data

### How to Exercise Your Rights
1. **In-App**: Use Profile → Settings → Privacy options
2. **Data Export**: Profile → Export Data
3. **Account Deletion**: Profile → Settings → Delete Account
4. **Email Request**: Contact us at privacy@trekapp.com

## Location Data and Permissions

### Location Permission
Trek requires location permission to:
- Record GPS coordinates during activities
- Display your route on a map
- Calculate distance and elevation

### When We Access Location
- **"While Using the App"**: Only when recording an activity
- **Foreground Only**: No background tracking (except during active recording)
- **User Control**: You can stop recording anytime

### Managing Location Permission
You can change location permissions in:
- iOS Settings → Trek → Location → "While Using the App" or "Never"

**Note**: Disabling location will prevent activity recording.

## Children's Privacy

Trek is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@trekapp.com, and we will delete the information.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have different data protection laws.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:
- Firebase/Google Cloud complies with GDPR and international data protection standards
- Data transfer agreements with service providers
- Adherence to Privacy Shield principles (where applicable)

## Cookies and Tracking

Trek does not use cookies or tracking pixels in the mobile app.

**Analytics**: We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage. This collects:
- App interactions
- Session duration
- Device information

You can opt-out of analytics in: Settings → Privacy → App Analytics → Off
